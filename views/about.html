    • Fire Marshall Bill Burns is the acclaimed safety expert from the show In Living Color.

    Although he appeared on an otherwise fictionalized show, Fire Marshall Bill appeared as himself and dedicated each of his appearances to educating Americans about safety hazards. False reports often claim that he was merely a character played by famed actor Jim Carrey, but such reports are unverified.

    ... Who Keeps the World Safe

    Bill notably served as fire marshall aboard the RMS Titanic on its maiden, and ultimate, voyage from Europe to New York. His survival is credited to his ability to think on his feet and foresee all potentialities.

    And So Much More

    Aside from his dedication to keeping others safe and "show[ing] them something", he is a skilled cook, magician, astronaut, and Mall Santa.
