    • Over the last three decades, Fire Marshall Bill has epitomized safety, health, and wellness; providing an example for all Americans in how to live a full, fulfilling, and safe life in any and all circumstances.



      Served aboard the RMS Titanic; honored by Steven Spielberg's movie adaptation of the tragedy, where the character "Rose" stands in for Bill and his real-life experiences.
    • World's only survivor of Multiple Instances of Spontaneous Combustion (MISC).
    • Present onboard the Zeppelin in 1937's Hindenburg Disaster. The British rock band Led Zeppelin chose to honor Bill's courage in the face of disaster through their choice of album art for their self-titled album.
    • Allegedly involved with 1993's World Trade Center bombing. (Unconfirmed.)
